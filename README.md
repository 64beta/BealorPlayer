## BealorPlayer (Local Video Playlist)

A small, browser-based tool that plays **local video files only** (files you select from your device) and builds a simple **playlist** UI. The player UI is powered by [Plyr](https://github.com/sampotts/plyr).

- **Live URL**: `https://bealorplayer.netlify.app`  

## What it does

- **Import a folder**: pick a directory and auto-load all videos inside (supported by Chromium-based browsers via `webkitdirectory`).
- **Add files**: pick one or more video files.
- **Playlist**: videos are listed on the right and sorted by filename (natural sort).


## How to use (local)

1. Open `index.html` in your browser, or serve it with any static server.
2. Click **IMPORT FOLDER** or **ADD FILES**.
3. Click an item in the playlist to play it.
